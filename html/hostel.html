Hostels Information

Sarabhai Hostel

Located in the heart of the campus, Sarabhai Hostel is known for its vibrant atmosphere and community events. With spacious rooms and modern facilities, it offers a comfortable stay for students. The hostel has an excellent common room for socializing, and the mess serves a variety of meals to cater to different tastes.

Aryabhatta Hostel

Aryabhatta Hostel is known for its peaceful environment and excellent study facilities. It offers rooms with comfortable furniture and study tables. The hostel has a well-maintained library and a common area perfect for group studies. The mess serves nutritious food that caters to the needs of students.

Chanakya Hostel

Chanakya Hostel offers a blend of comfort and modern amenities. With its centrally located rooms, it provides easy access to all campus facilities. The hostel has a recreational area with TV and gaming options, as well as a gym for fitness enthusiasts. Meals are served with a variety of options in the mess.

Boss Hostel

Boss Hostel is known for its spacious rooms and excellent management. The hostel has a variety of amenities including laundry services, high-speed internet, and a comfortable lounge area. It also has a cafeteria that offers delicious snacks and meals throughout the day.
