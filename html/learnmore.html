Empowering Students for a Bright Future

Overview

Chitkara University, established in 2002, is a leading private university located in Punjab, India. It is known for its commitment to providing world-class education, advanced research, and practical knowledge.

Academics

Chitkara University offers a wide range of academic programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels. The university ensures high-quality education and skill development.

Research and Innovation

Chitkara University emphasizes research and innovation across all disciplines. It fosters partnerships with industries to bring real-world solutions.

Our research centers are equipped to handle advanced research in engineering, health sciences, business, and more. Join us for groundbreaking work!

Global Exposure

Chitkara University offers global exposure to its students through partnerships with international universities and organizations. Students can participate in exchange programs and internships abroad.

Alumni Network

The alumni network of Chitkara University is strong and continuously growing. Graduates excel worldwide, offering mentorship and career opportunities to students.

Admissions

Chitkara University offers a seamless admissions process, with entrance exams and merit-based selections for various programs. Scholarships and financial aid are available to deserving students.
