About Tagore Library

The Tagore Library in our university is a spacious and well-stocked resource for students, offering a variety of books and study materials.The library houses an extensive collection of books across various disciplines, including literature, science, technology, history, arts, and more. It features both classic and contemporary works, catering to a diverse range of academic needs. In addition to physical books, the library offers access to digital resources, including e-books, journals, research papers, and databases like JSTOR, IEEE, and others. Tagore Library also hosts rare manuscripts, first editions, and historical archives related to Rabindranath Tagore and Indian culture.
